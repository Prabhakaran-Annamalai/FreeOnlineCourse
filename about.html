Providing Insight and Value :

Online Course Report was started after many of my own journeys through the jungle of online course providers. Even if many online courses are cheaper and more flexible than more traditional education, they’re time consuming and vary greatly in quality. That’s why Online Course Report was established. To maintain an independent and thoroughly researched voice on the best online courses.

Meet Your Editor :

Merrill Cook

Merrill has worked in online media for several years in roles ranging from data journalist, writer, editor, content strategist, course developer, WP developer, and data analyst. He’s acquired many of his skills through online courses, but not without pitfalls. That’s why he wanted to start Online Course Report, to provide quality commentary and a solid resource base for the online course-seeking community.

Advertising Policy :

Online Course Report strives to provide transparent, original, and research-based coverage of online education opportunities. No schools or online courses are placed in rankings due to payment. OnlineCourseReport does accept a small fee for connecting students with some potential online degree programs and courses. All locations in which OnlineCourseReport.com receives payment for connecting students to universities or courses are clearly labelled “Sponsored” and provide an explanation of our relationship with online education providers.
